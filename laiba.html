7 Wonders of the World

Picture Location Founded/Built by Year
The Great wall of China.
 Huairou District, China Qin Shi Huang, also called the First Emperor, united China. He masterminded the process of uniting the existing walls into one. Around 220 B.C.E.
Chichén Itzá El Castillo.
 Chichén Itzá, Yucatán state, Mexico. Chichén was founded about the 6th century ce, presumably by Maya peoples of the Yucatán Peninsula who had occupied the region since the Pre-Classic, or Formative, Period. Around 600-750 A.D.
Petra. the Khaznah.
 Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan It is thought that Al-Khazneh was built as a mausoleum and crypt at the beginning of the 1st century AD during the reign of Aretas IV Philopatris. 1st century AD
Sanctuary of Machu Picchu.
 Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru. Archaeologists believe that Machu Picchu was constructed as an estate for the Inca emperor Pachacuti.The Incas built the estate around 1450 but abandoned it a century later, at the time of the Spanish conquest. Around 1450 AD.
Christ the Redeemer.
 Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Corcovado It was created by French-Polish sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot. Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida sculpted the face. The statue was constructed between 1922 and 1931.
The Colosseum.
 Piazza del Colosseo, 1, 00184 Roma RM, Italy Construction of the Colosseum began under the Roman emperor Vespasian between 70 and 72 CE. The completed structure was dedicated in 80 CE by Titus, Vespasian's son and successor. AD 70–80; 1944 years ago
Taj Mahal.
 Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Pradesh 282001, India Ustad Ahmad Lahori also known as Ahmad Ma'mar Lahori was the chief Mughal architect during the reign of emperor Shah Jahan. He was responsible for the construction of several Mughal monuments, including the Taj Mahal in Agra and the Red fort in Delhi; both of which are World Heritage sites. The construction started in 1632 AD and completed in 1648 AD.
